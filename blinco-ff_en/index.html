WOBBLERS

About Us...


Blinker Co. was founded in 1994. Except producing and exporting fishing floats, the company also imports and distributes fishing gear. Through the retail facility in "Ribarski Raj - Lineaeffe" in Niš, as well as through the Blinker OnLine store, we offer fishermen in Serbia fishing accessories from programs of well-known brands, of which we are the exclusive importers and distributors for the territory of Serbia. After 2000, we found an open space for our products, both domestically and in the European Union market, because we offer high quality products under registered customer brands, at a very reasonable price and with reliable service to our customers.

We continuously export 95% of our production to customers in Italy, the United Kingdom, Spain, Portugal, France, Croatia, Bulgaria ... Our floats are made of high quality materials produced in the European Union. Finishing painting is done with precision and quality is constantly improved with our own research and with constant application of new production solutions, techniques and new design. Two basic features of our production are: high quality, with full guarantee and response to all our customers's requirements. We sincerely hope that we can meet the demand of every buyer and the fishing market, for mutual satisfaction and mutual benefit. Please feel welcome to visit our Website, browse our products and request any additional information.
