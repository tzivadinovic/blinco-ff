FISHING FLOATS

Our floats are made of high quality materials produced in the European Union. The final painting is done with precision and quality is constantly improved with our own research and with constant application of new production solutions, techniques and new design.

WOBBLERS

BLINCO wobblers are the result of a combination of years of fishing experience, with 25 years of experience in fishing gear manufacturing. All wobblers are made of unbreakable platinum, POLYCARBONATE, carefully bonded with ULTRA-SOUND welding, hand painted and finalized.

FISHING RIGS
AND OTHER EQUIPMENT

Finished fishing rigs are complete with the highest quality Lineaeffe floats, premium Samurai nylon and Izumo Japanese carbon fishing rod. Each rig is individually packed in a bag, with marked details (hook size, nylon thickness and length and float size).
